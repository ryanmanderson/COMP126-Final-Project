About Us

Mission

Building an understanding of emerging technologies and expanding their benefits in our communities.

History

Carolina Tech Explorers was founded as “Carolina Glass Explorers” in 2013 with the mission of using Google Glass to help people with disabilities. Since then, we have expanded our mission to include more technologies and increased community opportunities. This expansion drove us to choose our new organization name.

Goals

  • To use technology and design programs to help those in the UNC community and surrounding local communities
  • To educate people about ways that technology is able to do good in the world
